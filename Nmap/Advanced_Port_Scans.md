# Nmap: Advanced Port Scans – TryHackMe

## Objective
The objective of this room was to understand advanced Nmap scanning techniques and how different scan types work for port analysis.

## Environment
- Platform: TryHackMe
- Tool: Nmap
- Operating System: Linux
- Access Method: Web-based terminal / AttackBox

## Concepts & Skills Practiced
- TCP SYN scan
- TCP Connect scan
- UDP scan
- NULL, FIN, and Xmas scans
- Stealth scanning techniques
- Understanding filtered vs closed ports
- Analyzing advanced scan results
